About

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at IMPA, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, working under the supervision of Professor Claudio Landim.

My research interests lie mainly in Probability Theory and its connections to other areas, including interacting particle systems, partial differential equations, optimal transport, statistics and machine learning. At the moment I am mostly interested in deep learning and its applications.

You can find me at Office 429 at IMPA, or reach me at mangi.joao@impa.br.
