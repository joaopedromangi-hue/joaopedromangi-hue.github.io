About me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at IMPA, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, working under the supervision of professor Claudio Landim.

My research interests are mainly in Probability Theory and its connections to other areas, such as interacting particle systems, partial differential equations, optimal transport, statistics and machine learning. At the moment I am mostly interested in Deep Learning and its applications.
